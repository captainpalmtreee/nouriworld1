About Nouri

Nouri is a Certified Personal Trainer and Public Figure who has dedicated his life to changing lives by sharing his creative workouts, life experiences, and words of wisdom. Nouri’s training is a mixture of bodybuilding and boxing, strength training, metabolic conditioning and functional training. Nouri’s ambition is to motivate others to take that first step because you never know where that first step can take you. Great things are always on the other side of fear, and the aim is to support you until you get there.

His world-class mindset and methods will assist you in being the best version of yourself. The key to a healthy body and physique is being educated and staying motivated. With Nouri’s knowledge and experience, you’ll be on the right path to a better you, if you are genuinely ready to take action. Nouri has learnt how to achieve results fast by merely being disciplined and patient with his approach to nutrition and understanding the human physiology. You must first change your mind and start with a positive outlook in order to achieve a happy, sustainable, and healthy lifestyle.
To be an achiever, it’s all about a winning mindset and taking ACTION.

Whatever your health and fitness goals may be, Nouri works with you every step of the way. Through science, experience, and world-class mindset, we’ll have you setting and breaking personal records and help you get the body you’ve always dreamed of. Your results are my successes.

Nouri’s Mission

All his life he’ve been an athlete. From boxing to Fitness.
After an injury in boxing, he used fitness to get him through that transition period which definitely wasn’t easy. His life took a turn and he struggled mentally and physically. His life was in jeopardy, that was his motivation to begin his journey. He didn’t really know what the future held, but he always knew he had the choice to put some work in each day. Fitness saved his life. The idea that so many things in life are out of our control, but we get to choose to do something to get better each day. He now have the ability to help others do the same.
